Getting started

What does CallCanvas AI actually do?

CallCanvas AI is a territory research tool for outside sales reps. You enter your city or ZIP and what you sell. We return a ranked call list of real local businesses with owner/decision-maker names, direct phone numbers, and personalized opening lines — in under 5 minutes.

It is not a CRM, dialer, call recording tool, or coaching platform. It’s the research step that happens before you start dialing.

How do I start a free trial?

Click Start Free Trial anywhere on the homepage. You’ll go through Stripe checkout. After payment, you’ll land on a welcome page that takes you straight to research. You won’t be charged until day 8 — and you can cancel anytime from your account.

What if I close my browser? How do I get back in?

Easy — visit callcanvasai.com/login.html, enter the email you signed up with, and we’ll log you in (no password). Your email is also remembered in your browser, so most of the time you can just bookmark callcanvasai.com/research.html.

Using territory research

How long does a research session take?

Most reps complete a 50-business call sheet in under 5 minutes. The actual AI generation takes about 20 seconds — the rest is you scanning the results and downloading the CSV.

Can I research just one company instead of 50?

Yes. In the “Specific product” field you can enter a single company name (e.g., “Wendy’s on Galena Boulevard”) and the AI will return research focused on that one business. This is useful when you’re sitting in the parking lot and want context before walking in.

What industries does it work for?

It’s designed for outside reps in: insurance, financial services, B2B technology, telecom, business phones, payroll services, commercial cleaning, security systems, and other field-sales-heavy categories. It works in any U.S. city or ZIP code.

How accurate is the contact data?

The AI pulls from publicly available business records, websites, and directories. Most owner names and main phone numbers are accurate, but business records change frequently — we recommend a quick verification call before a sales conversation. Always check do-not-call lists (TCPA, state-level) before dialing.

How do I download my results?

Every research output has two buttons at the top: Download CSV (for importing into your CRM or spreadsheet) and Print field notes (formatted for the clipboard you carry in the truck).

Troubleshooting

I get “Access Denied” when I try to run research

This usually means one of three things:

1. You haven’t saved your email in this browser. Visit /login.html and enter the email you used at checkout.

2. Your subscription was canceled or your card was declined. Visit /account.html to check.

3. You’re using a different browser or incognito mode where your email isn’t saved.

The AI chat (Alex) isn’t responding

Alex is our AI sales rep. If she’s slow or unresponsive, refresh the page and try again. If the issue persists, email support@callcanvasai.com.

Research generation timed out

Research generation has a 26-second timeout. If it times out (rare), just hit “Build My Call Sheet” again — the second attempt almost always succeeds. If it consistently fails, email us with the city/product you tried.

Privacy & data

What data do you collect?

Email (for your account) and the queries you run (city, product). Payments are handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your card details. Full details in our Privacy Policy.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. Email support@callcanvasai.com and we’ll delete your account and all associated records within 30 days.

Are research outputs watermarked?

Yes. Every CSV and printed list includes a small watermark with your account email and the generation timestamp. This helps us track misuse if generated lists are shared outside an active subscription.
