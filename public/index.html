FAQ

Everything reps ask before they sign up.

Do I need to be technical to use this? +

No. If you can copy and paste, you can run CallCanvas AI. The Master Prompt is plain English. Open Claude, paste it in, answer a few questions about your territory, and the output drops into the spreadsheet we give you.

Does it really run on the free Claude tier? +

Yes. The prompts fit inside Claude's free daily limits. A typical 50-company session stays well within the free window. You never need a paid Anthropic subscription or API key.

How is this different from SPOTIO or SalesRabbit? +

SPOTIO and SalesRabbit require a team of 5+ reps and cost $395–$645/month minimum. CallCanvas AI is $59/month for a single rep, no team, no demo, works in minutes. Built for the solo outside rep, not the enterprise.

How long does a research session take? +

Most reps complete a 50-company run in 75–105 minutes on their first try. By session three, consistently under 90 minutes — with a ranked call list and decision-maker names ready to go.

What industries does it work for? +

Insurance, financial services, B2B technology, telecom, commercial services, payroll and HR. Any outside sales rep who drives a territory and cold-approaches businesses.

What if I want to cancel? +

Cancel anytime, no questions asked. Month-to-month always. No annual contracts, no cancellation fees.
